Hi readers, today we'll be sampling the poem 'Jack n Jill', a poem about a boy and a girl that went up to the hills to get some water and had a little disaster right after. At the end of the day, they both rose up above their little diaster. And the poem goes thus;



Jack n Jill went up the hill to fetch a pail of water

Jack fell down and broke his crown, And Jill came tumbling after



Up Jack got and home did trot, As fast as he could caper,

He went to bed to mend his Crown, With vinegar and brown paper.



Conclusion

We can all see from the poem that Jack didn't let his fall take the best of his crown, he got up and instead of going home to whine about it, he mended his crown. No matter what problems you face, you can make the best of it and turn it into a positive for yourself and no matter how many times you fall, get back up even stronger!!
