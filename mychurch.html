We at C&S Unification Church Of Nigeria Oke Iye Society welcome you to our web page and we pray that the goodness of the Lord overflows in your life. We are fully covinced that all who believe will surely be saved.

Established 70 years ago and still bringing you the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ, we welcome you to join us at our church auditorium and witness the grace, love and mercies of God in your life.

Everyone is welcome regardless of color, religion or belief, its the house of the LORD.

Prayer Points
  1. Thank God for His faithfulness in our lives, in our families and in the Church of God. Thank God for His love and protection over us.
  2. Ask God to renew the anointing of the Holy spirit in your life so that you can continue to bear fruits.
  3. Arise oh God and fight my battle, separate me from every inherited battles in Jesus Name.
  4. My Joy this year must not be cut short. Let every spoiler at the edge of my success be arrested in Jesus Name.
  5. O Lord anoint me with fresh oil. Grant me the power to excel in every area of my life.

May Our Prayers be answered in Jesus Mighty Name. Amen.
